Welcome to the NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF) Playbook – a resource in support of the AI RMF.

The Playbook includes suggested actions, references, and supplementary guidance that stakeholders can take to achieve outcomes for “Map” and “Govern” – two of the four proposed functions in the AI RMF. Draft material for the other two functions, Measure and Manage, will be released at a later date.
